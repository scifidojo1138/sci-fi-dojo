What It Is

Remember browsing for a movie?

Sci-Fi Dojo is a membership-based physical media rental club located inside a local space in Asbury Park. Think of it as a video store, but curated by someone who has spent years building a collection worth sharing.

It started with lending movies to friends out of a personal collection. It kept growing. So did the lending. Eventually it made more sense to make it official — a proper club, with proper discs, for people who take movies seriously.

Every title in the collection was chosen on purpose. Blu-ray, DVD, and 4K UHD. Sci-fi and beyond. Films that reward a real watch on a real screen with the lights low.

How It Works

Simple as it should be

01
Become a Member
Membership is handled directly. Reach out and we’ll get you set up.
02
Browse the Cabinet
Come by the location and browse the cabinet. Something will grab you. It always does.
03
Check It Out
Scan the disc with your phone to check it out to your account. No counter. No waiting. Self-service checkout.
04
Watch It Right
Take it home. Watch it on a real screen. Return it when you are done and grab something new.
